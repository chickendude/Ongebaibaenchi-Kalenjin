# Lesson 14

**Moning'otiot ak kwondo 5** – Husband and wife 5

### Dialog

| Speaker       | Kalenjin                                                | English                                                                     |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Bakule | bo ngo simoit ne bole en sum, u ter cheiwet manenyun | Whose phone is making noise in the room, it's like the sound is different not mine |
| Moning'otiot | nenyun. Ngoro chelangat koibwan. "Chelangat\! Chelangat\! Bomuru\! Miten chelangat yon i? | It's mine. Where's chelangat to bring for me. Chelangat\! Chelangat\! Bomuru\! Is chelangat there? |
| Bomuru | achicha momiten. Kasor bendo en soket. Imoche iyokte? Mangen ngot ko chogu. | No, she's isn't here. She went to bring meat at the butchery. Do you want to send her? I don't know if she'll hurry |
| Bakule | Kalenjin i bakule. Kwondo nebo Makibior ne kakowis chepyosok metowek. Kayam keguur tuiyet nebo kokwet kitigon chepyosok. Motinye tegisto tugul. | I tell you Bakule. Makibior's wife is the one who is corrupting women's heads. It's enough to call for a village meeting to advise women. They all have no respect anymore |
| Moning'otiot | Iman bakule. Kakwong' kityo obot Chelangat. Nyolu kecheng kiptayatab kokwet kotet ak kobarasta tuiyonoto. | Very true Bakule. I just wondered Chelangat's mother. We should look for village elder fo him to plan and advertise that meeting |
| Bomuru | Bomuru\! Tos iame bendap kechiriet anan inye iame artet kityo? Kasor chelangat nebo kechiriet. Kichome noto en koi amun tinye mwanik chechang. | Bomuru. Do you eat sheep's meat or goat only? Chelangat went to bring sheep's meat. We like it in this house because it had as a lot of fat. |
| Moning'otiot | Aame banyek tugul. Achame missing noto bo kechiriet amun puspus yon kakibel koyam. | I eat all types of meat. I like that for sheep because it's tender when roasted. |
| Bomuru | iame banyek tugul i? Agot nebo chito? Kaaiywein Bomuru. | You take all types of meat? Even human meat? I now afraid of you Bomuru |
| Moning'otiot | e e. Maame nebo chito. tomo koit chelangat en soket i? | No. I don't eat human meat. Chelangat hasn't come from the butchery? |
| Bomuru | Tomo. Mangen kanam nee. Tos kakoit amun kawo keny | Not yet. I don't know what caught her up. She should have arrived because she went long ago |
| Bakule | okas o. U mi ne wache. Aikwek Chelangat lakwetab bik. Ongerwai kesorun | You listen. It's like there's someone wailing. Oh my Chelangat people's child. Let's run and save her |

### Vocabulary

1. **Mwanik** – Fats
2. **Kwong'** – (Literally, be stupid) Get surprised
3. **Iywei** – Be afraid
4. **Chei** – Make sound
5. **Sorun** – Save
