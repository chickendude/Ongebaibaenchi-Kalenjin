### Dialog

| Speaker       | Kalenjin                                                | English                                                                     |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Kwondo | Apirin nguni, lakwani. Tomo inai tuguk chebo ngwony. Iming'in. | I will beat you now, (this) child. You haven't known things in this world. You're still young. |
| Chelangat | Aming'in iman ayani. Manyolu iyai tuguk che u chu. | True I am young I agree. You must not do things like this. |
| Moning'otiot | Kwonyinyun, kalya rani. Kiigas rotek chebo kwonyik en kokwet. Aikwek kakobar konyon rotek. | My wife, what's up today, you've had women gossip in the village. Gossip has destroyed our home |
| Kwondo | Ui cheng chepkinyariniguk inye. Mechutu rani koi mang'alin. | You go look for your prostitutes. You'll not get in this house today, am not lying to you |
| Chelangat | Iyo metinye tegisto akinye. Ami yu ak imwoe ngalek che u chon i? | And you mum you don't have respect. I am here and you're speaking such words |
| Kwondo | Manden Chelangat. Iyomisan lakwet ne ming'in ne kochuchuni amut i? Tomo koit chegab kinet kutung'oik | Chelangat get out. Can a young child who was breastfeeding yesterday advise me? The breast milk has not yet reached (your) knees. |
| Chelangat | Tos ingen moning'otiot ko inendet metit en kapchi? Nyolu iborchi tegisto. Kaigai yat kot. | Do you know a husband is the head of the family? You must show him respect. Please open the house |
| Moning'otiot | Chelangat, awendi asom bakule kot aru. Nichu sukaruk ak busiek mi yu kakopir ropta. | Chelangat, am going to request a house to sleep from bakule. Here is sugar and flour it has been rained on. |
| Chelangat | Achicha matiwe, nyolu koyat kot iyo. Inye i metit nebo kot. Kaigai matiwe amun rorechin bakule neng'ung' kole kawonin kwondo. | No, do not go. Mum must open the door. You're the head of this home. Please don't go because your bakule will laugh at you that a woman chased you |
| Moning'otiot | Mwae bukuit ne tilil kole, bolin kwondo ne berber, abusat ak manae ki. Katil awo amun mie bomuru. Mamache inee rotek kou komet. | The Bible says, A woman of foolishness is boisterous, She has a lack of understanding and knows nothing. I have decided to go because bomuru is good. She doesn't want gossip like your mother |
| Kwondo | Ui. Imunye metit nenyun missing ndo iwendi betusiek chechang | Go, my head will relax a lot if you go for many days |
| Moning'otiot | Amwochini boisyek kobwa kokas ngalechu. Nyolu kechigil kundit neng'ung' anan koichamcham nyasoret. | I will tell old men to come listen to this. Your brain should be checked or have you tasted drugs |
| Chelangat | Ui kityo icheng kot neirue imuch komie iyo mutai. Masise ra. | Just go look for a house you will sleep maybe mum will be good tomorrow. She's not getting quiet today. |
| Moning'otiot | Kongoi missing lakwani. Ndo u lagok tugul inye, baibaitu kapchi tugul. | Thanks much this child. If all the children are like you, all families will be happy |

### Vocabulary

1. **bakule** – Person you were circumcised together.
2. **bomuru** – Wife to your brother, the person of your age group and to your bakule
3. **nyasoret** – drug  
   Can refer to any type of drug: bhang, cocaine, etc.
4. **chigil** – check, investigate
5. **kundit** – brain  
   Also called:
    1. **takungut** – brain
6. **yomis** – advise
7. **bukuit ne tilil** – Bible ("clean book")
8. **tegisto** – respect
9. **kinet** – breast
10. **chepkinyariat** – lady, prostitute
    1. **nyer** – to rape
