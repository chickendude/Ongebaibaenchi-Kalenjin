## Lesson 13

**Moning'otiot ak kwondo 4** – Husband and wife 4

### Dialog

| Speaker       | Kalenjin                                                | English                                                                     |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Moning'otiot | Iyo. Mi ng'o koi, Bakule ngoro inye? Bomuru nini tondet kibelelit. | Who's in this house, Bakule, where are you? Bomuru here's a very great visitor. |
| Bomuru | Bomuru, kalyan iwendoti en ropta kemoi. Kagobirin ropta ak ibotani missing. Nyon ko iyu mat. | Bomuru why are you walking in the rain at night? You have been rained on and you're shivering a lot. Come in warm yourself. |
| Moning'otiot | Kongoi Bomuru. Mongen kanam nee obot Chelangat. Kopiran ropta ak mayat kot. | Thanks Bomuru. I don't know what's caught Chelangat's mother. I have been rained on and she didn't open the house. |
| Bomuru | Obot Chelangat kokonam chorwandit ak Recho. Ingen Recho chome kobut korik chebo bik. | Chelangat's mother has started friendship with Recho. You know Recho loves to destroy people's homes. |
| Moning'otiot | Recho non chome kimainik anan Recho kwondo nebo Makibior. U chome tuwan rotek? Momi ne kaigai en ichek. | That Recho who loves drunkards or Recho the wife of Makibior. It's like both love gossip. No one is better between them. |
| Bakule | Itagat missing, Bakule. Komi sum alieldoi. Nechu imwoe, Bakule? Kawonun Bomuru kalyan? | Most welcome Bakule. I was in the room lying. What are you saying Bakule, Bomuru chased you why? |
| Moning'otiot | Kanyasan tuguk che kayai? Komi kotab Jelimo achobe ngecheret ne kokail. Kaitu gaa kolole obot Chelangat. | I am surprised with what she did. I was in Jelimo's house making a broken chair. (When) I arrived home Chelangat's mother was fuming. |
| Chelangat | Iyo. |  |
| Moning'otiot | Chelangat, kesibuan i? Kayat ou komet kurgat? Komi ak ng'o inendet bet? | Chelangat, you followed me? When did your mother open the door. She was with who during the day? |
| Chelangat | Komi ak Recho kwondab Makibior. Kokaba tuiyet nebo saet yoto. | She was with Recho, Makibior's wife. They went to a prayer meeting there. |
| Bakule | Ya kwondab Makibior missing. Inendet ne inete Bomuru che yach. Nan acheng'un ingoroik iwal che twonen. Kibendi mutai kenyorun inee. Chelangat, ui chiket itoret komet nebo koi okwany. Nan keam logoiwek ak Bakule. | Makibior's wife is bad. She is the one teaching Bomuru bad things. Let me look for some clothes for you to change out of these wet ones. We shall go tomorrow to meet her. Chelangat, go to the kitchen and help your mother of this house to cook. Let us discuss \["eat these news"\] with Bakule. |

### Vocabulary

1. **Logoiwek** – News
2. **Chiket** – Kitchen
3. **Kanyasan** – I got surprised – From the word 'nyas' meaning sudden or surprise. The word nyasutiet come from this word
4. **Ng'echeret** – Chair
5. **Sib** – Follow
6. **Kolole** – Burning – From the word 'lal' meaning to burning
7. **Kibelelit** – Big/great – Used to describe something big. It's a word showing that something is very big/great
8. **Nyorun** – To meet
9. **Yoto** – There
10. **Saet** – Prayer
11. **Alieldoi** – I am lying in bed (relax)
